About Us

This is the future of healthcare service online. Our future is here.You can try it with new featuring emergency care service. Times have changed and so have the trends of health care industry, which is estimated to be the third most important industrial sector in the world. OHRC is a part of this changing global health care scenario that is striving to provide best Medicare at affordable charges. The origin of OHRC dates back to the futuristic vision of late Alhaj Moulana Abdul Wahed Owaisi, a great scholar, and an astute politician. In 1996, his dream of creating a world class 1050 bedded super specialty hospital that matches its resources with the raising expectations of the levels of health care eventually became a reality
